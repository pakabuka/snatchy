Part One  —  Privacy Policy

What This Policy Covers

Legal Desk  |  Compliance Bureau

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, retention, and disclosure of information gathered when you play $natchy, a multiplayer market-floor game distributed as a mobile application. By entering your name and joining or creating a market, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms described herein.

$natchy is designed to be minimal in its data footprint. We collect only what is genuinely necessary to make the game work across multiple devices simultaneously. Nothing more is gathered; nothing superfluous is retained.

This policy applies to all versions of the $natchy application and any related platforms or distribution channels through which the game is made available. It does not apply to third-party services linked from within the app except as described below.

Information Collected

Data Inventory  |  Full Disclosure

When you use $natchy, the following categories of information are processed:

✦   Data Categories
  • Category A — IdentityThe player name you enter at the start of your session. This is a name of your own choosing and need not be your real name.
  • Category B — Market ActivityThe market code you create or join; your score as accumulated during rounds; your ready status and exit status within a market.
  • Category C — Gameplay EventsRecords of cash note and debt note interactions — specifically, which notes were collected and when — used to prevent duplicate collection and synchronise state.
  • Category D — Technical MetadataTimestamps of joining, readiness changes, and collection events; a randomly generated device session ID used to identify your player record within a market. This ID is not linked to your device identity or any persistent identifier.

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, IP addresses, behavioural advertising profiles, or any biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Limitation  |  Strict Construction

All data collected is used exclusively for the following purposes, and for no others:

  • Creating and joining multiplayer markets in real time.
  • Synchronising game state — scores, note positions, readiness — across all connected devices simultaneously.
  • Displaying the roster, scores, and player statuses within a market.
  • Preventing the same note from being collected twice through atomic transaction checks.
  • Determining round results and the final winner of each game.
  • Enabling the host to start and advance rounds, and to reset the game for a new session.

We do not use your information for profiling, targeted advertising, analytics resale, or any purpose unrelated to operating the game.

Visibility to Other Players

Transparency Notice  |  Fellow Bankers
"Your name is your brand on the market floor. Choose it wisely."

Certain data is visible to other participants in the same market. This is inherent to the multiplayer nature of the game. The following fields are visible to co-players:

  • Your player name as entered.
  • Your current ready status (ready or not ready).
  • Your score at all times during and after rounds.
  • Whether you have exited the market.

Players in other markets, or users not in your current market, cannot see any of your data. Markets are fully isolated from one another.

Important: Do not use sensitive personal information — your full name, address, identification numbers, or any other private data — as your player name. Your chosen name will be visible to strangers.

Sharing & Service Providers

Disclosure Register  |  Third Parties

$natchy uses Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service, to store and synchronise all gameplay data in real time. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's own privacy policies and terms of service.

Firebase processes the data described in this policy solely to provide the infrastructure necessary for the game to function. No other third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data broker services are integrated into $natchy.

✦   Third-Party Notice

Firebase Firestore is the sole third-party data processor. We do not sell, rent, lease, licence, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any other party for any commercial purpose whatsoever.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or binding governmental authority, in which case we will disclose only the minimum required.

Data Retention

Records Management  |  Archival Policy

Gameplay data — market records, player names, scores, and note interaction logs — is retained only for as long as is reasonably necessary to operate the service, resolve disputes, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Market records are not automatically deleted at the conclusion of a game. They may be retained for a reasonable period to facilitate debugging, fraud prevention, and service improvement. However, given the pseudonymous and limited nature of the data, retention does not present a material privacy risk in ordinary circumstances.

If you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your session, please contact the app operator using the information provided at the end of this document. Deletion requests will be handled promptly and in good faith.

✦   Session IDs

The randomly generated player ID used during your session is created fresh each time you enter the name screen. It is not persistent across app installations or device resets and cannot be used to track you over time.

Security Measures

Protection Desk  |  Technical Safeguards

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information processed in connection with $natchy. These include:

  • Transmission of all data over encrypted HTTPS connections via Firebase's secure SDK.
  • Firestore security rules that restrict read and write access to participants within a given market.
  • Atomic Firestore transactions to prevent race conditions in note collection — the same transaction mechanism that protects the game also protects data integrity.
  • No storage of sensitive personal data that would constitute a high-risk breach if exposed.

No system is perfectly secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats. In the event of a material breach affecting your privacy rights, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Minors Protection  |  COPPA Compliance

$natchy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game involves financial themes — cash, debt, competition — suitable for teenagers and adults.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the application, please contact the app operator immediately. Upon verification, we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our systems.

Users in jurisdictions where the age of digital consent is higher than 13 — including, for example, certain EU member states where the age is 16 — should comply with their local requirements.
